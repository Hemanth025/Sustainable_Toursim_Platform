About EcoJourney

EcoJourney is an AI-Powered Sustainable Tourism Platform designed to revolutionize the way we travel. Our mission is to make eco-friendly travel accessible, enjoyable, and impactful for everyone.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable tourism practices by providing intelligent travel recommendations, environmental impact assessments, and cultural engagement tools that respect both nature and local communities.

Our Approach

Using rule-based AI systems aligned with ISO 14001 environmental standards and UNWTO sustainable tourism guidelines, we calculate carbon footprints, recommend eco-friendly destinations, and connect travelers with local economies.

Your Privacy

Your data is protected with industry-standard encryption and multi-factor authentication. We follow GDPR compliance and never share your personal information with third parties.

Capstone Phase 2 Project

This platform is developed as part of an academic capstone project, showcasing the integration of sustainable tourism principles with modern web technologies.
